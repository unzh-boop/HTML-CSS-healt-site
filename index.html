Health is your best friend. And your protector.

The word health refers to a state of complete emotional and physical well-being.
Healthcare exists to help people maintain this optimal state of health. According to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC),
healthcare costs in the United States were $3.5 trillionTrusted Source in 2017.
However, despite this expenditure, people in the U.S. have a lower life expectancy than people in other developed countries.

Good health is central to handling stress and living a longer, more active life. In this blog, we explain the meaning of good health, the types of health a person needs to consider, and how to preserve good health.
